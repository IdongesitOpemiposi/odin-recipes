JOLLOF SPAGHETTI

Description

Jollof spaghetti is a simple meal popular in Nigerian homes. It is your go to for a quick delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • 2 satchets of tomato paste
  • 4 pieces of seasoning cube
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 kg of beef(any protein of your choice will do)
  • 1 medium sized bulb of onion
  • 1 tablespoon of soy oil(use oil of choice)

Steps

  1. Cut beef into small bite sized pieces, add 2 seasoning cubes and a teaspoonful of salt with some chopped onions and bring to boil.
  2. Boil a litre of water in another pot and add the spaghetti with a teaspoon of salt and leave to boil until it's almost soft
  3. Drain the water from the spaghetti and keep aside in a bowl.
  4. Heat up a pan and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  5. Pour chopped onions and tomato paste into the pot and stir for two minutes.
  6. Add beef with stock, stir and allow to cook till little water is left.
  7. Add mixed vegetable and stir (optional).
  8. Pour boiled spaghetti into the pot and stir.
  9. Serve hot with a cold fruit drink.
