PORRIDGE OATS

DESCRIPTION

Porridge oats makes a highly nutritious breakfast made from oats. It is ideal for evey one which makes it a family favourite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup of milk (optional)
  • sweetener of choice(optional)
  • fruits/toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Put one cup of oats into a pot.
  2. Pour 2 cups of water into the pot.
  3. Place pot on lowest heat, cover and allow to cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Stir and allow to cook for a minute.
  5. Turn into a bowl and add a cup of milk and honey and stir.
  6. Serve with any fruits or toppings of choice.
