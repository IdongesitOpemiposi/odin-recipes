OMELETTE

Description

Omelette is a meal made from eggs. It is quick and easy to make and can be adapted to suit your preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized chicken eggs
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil(or any oil of your choice)
  • half cup of chopped vegetable of choice(green, red, yellow bell peppers, spring onions, carrot, etc).

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl and whisk till it lathers
  2. Add a pinch of salt and whisk
  3. Add half cup of chopped vegetables and whisk
  4. Heat up a frying pan on low heat
  5. Pour 2 tablespoons of olive oil into the pan
  6. Pour the whisked egg with vegetables into the pan and allow to cook for two minutes
  7. Flip when the edges turn brown and allow to cook for a minute
  8. Serve with any toppings of choice
